Key modules

What the first version of Youality includes.

This prototype imagines a v1 focused on solo users. Future iterations unlock coach-client spaces and shared maps.

Core space

My Day · Navigation

A lightweight daily check-in where you update your energy, pick your focus, and write intentions that your future self will actually read.

System view

Life Map · Web of Worlds

A bird’s-eye map of your life areas, projects, and roles, using metaphors like Garden of Life and Web of Worlds instead of endless lists.

Rhythm

Inner & outer cycles

Weekly and monthly rhythm views that sync your plans with your actual energy patterns and seasons of life — not just arbitrary dates.

Memory

Library of Self

A structured archive of insights, decisions, and “breadcrumbs” so you can see how you’re evolving over time without digging through notes.

Celebration

Victory Arches

Visual milestones that mark important transitions: finishing a project, shifting an identity, or completing a personal quest.

Future

Coach & companion view

A future layer where a coach can see a client’s map, rhythms, and intentions and support them with context — without micromanaging.
