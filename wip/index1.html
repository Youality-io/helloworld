About

A living map for a real, evolving life.

Not another productivity app. Youality is a way of seeing — and steering — your life: identity, vision, projects, habits, and energy in one coherent system.

Most tools force you to choose between structure and soul. Either it’s rigid planning with no resonance, or it’s inspiring but impossible to track.

Youality is being built as a personal navigation system that honors both: the part of you that loves clarity, and the part that moves with intuition, seasons, and feeling.

Map identity, vision & projects in one place

Track energy, not just tasks
Why build this?
Fragmented tools 7+ apps

Most of us juggle calendars, notes, task managers, docs, and mind maps. Youality aims to be the place where everything meaningful connects — and still feels light, human, and alive.

Principles

Built for creators, not just “users”.

Under the hood, Youality blends system thinking, coaching, and design. On the surface, it should feel like a calm, playful cockpit for your life.

Pillar 01

Creativity + Structure

Your life isn’t a spreadsheet. Youality lets you build systems that feel like you: visual metaphors, life areas as “worlds”, and timelines that match your rhythm — all anchored in solid planning logic.

Pillar 02

Identity-anchored planning

Instead of chasing random goals, you start from: Who am I becoming? From there, you map projects, habits, and experiments that actually serve that direction.

Pillar 03

Energy-aware navigation

Some days are “launch mode”. Others are “integration”. Youality tracks your inner weather and helps you match plans to actual capacity — not wishful thinking.

Pillar 04

Coach-ready by design

The long-term vision includes a shared cockpit for coaches and clients. For now, early versions will already be friendly to frameworks, rituals, and reflection practices you love.

How it works

From foggy to navigable in three layers.

Exact features will evolve with early users, but the backbone will stay the same: see clearly, design intentionally, and move in rhythm.

Layer 01 — Clarity: See your current reality

Youality starts by helping you map where you are right now:

  • Life areas as a “garden”, “city”, or system that actually resonates with you.
  • Key projects, commitments, and energetic pulls in each area.
  • Inner weather: what feels draining, alive, or simply unclear.

From there, patterns emerge — and decisions get easier.

This landing is a living preview, not a promise of final UI. Early access members will co-create what these layers look and feel like in practice.
